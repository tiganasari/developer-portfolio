Quarantined with Cats

Part photography collection, part memoir, Quarantined with Cats (QWC) is a story about a black cat and two people who grew to love her. Taken place primarily during the 1st wave of UK London in 2020, QWC is an attempt to stay light and sane during the difficult times of COVID-19.
